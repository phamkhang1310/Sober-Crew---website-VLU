OUR NARRATIVE

Framing the Soul since 2024

Sober Crew was born out of a collective desire to push the boundaries of visual media in Vietnam. Located in Ho Chi Minh City, we started as a passionate group of visual artists and have grown into a full-scale creative production house.

We believe that every story has a core frequency—a soul. Our mission is to capture that frequency and project it onto the screen with absolute technical precision and artistic elegance. Whether it is a luxury brand commercial, a music video, or a studio photoshoot, we align every frame to fit your brand's unique identity.
